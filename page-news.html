In Honor of Charles D. Shook
  • Written by Jolene Meusburger
  • Tagged as Company News

Bieser, Greer & Landis lost our most long standing member on March, 16, 2015 at the age of 87. Charles D. Shook (“Charlie”) started with the firm upon his admission to the practice of law in 1952. He ceased active practice in 2014 after 62 years. He had a great mind and the firm relied on his counsel for many years as he took the role of Senior Partner. His calm demeanor, smile, and wit will be sorely missed. We are all better lawyers and people as a result of Charlie’s influence. Rest in Peace, Old Friend.

Attorney Trisha Duff joins the Law Firm
  • Written by Jolene Meusburger
  • Tagged as Company News

Bieser, Greer & Landis is pleased to announce that attorney Trisha Duff has joined the law firm effective November 1, 2013. Duff brings twenty years of family law experience to the firm. Previously a solo practitioner, Duff represented clients in matters of family law, juvenile law, criminal law, and as a labor union representative.
