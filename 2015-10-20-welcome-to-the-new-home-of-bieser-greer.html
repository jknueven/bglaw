Welcome to the new home of Bieser Greer

  • Written by Erin Paulson
  • Tagged as Company News

You’ve probably noticed a few changes around here lately.

We’ve worked hard over the past few months to create a new website as polished and professional as our firm. This site launch is a part of a larger effort to rebrand Bieser Greer, previously Bieser, Greer & Landis. We even have a new logo – one that better represents our firm’s strengths.

The goal of our rebranding initiative was to update our image in a way that reflects not only our deep and storied history, but our status as a contemporary law firm looking to the future.

Our firm may have a fresh, new look, but one thing that has stayed the same is our commitment to our customers through quality service – building relationships with our clients and seeking efficient solutions to their problems. We remain devoted to a tradition of exceptional service.

Bieser Greer’s Dayton roots extend back more than 160 years. We’re happy to call the Gem City our home, and hope to continue to serve it for another century and a half – and beyond.

We encourage you to peruse our new website – we think you’ll find some great information about our breadth of legal services.

In the meantime, feel free to get in touch and let us know how we can be of service. We look forward to hearing from you.
