Professional Experience
Primary areas of practice
Criminal Defense, White Collar Crime, Litigation, Military Law, Government Regulatory Actions & Security Clearance Investigations
Mr. Fleisher focuses his practice in the areas of white collar criminal defense and federal and state regulatory enforcement actions. He also has extensive experience handling matters involving state criminal law, military law, security clearance investigations and hearings, asset forfeiture, and business disputes. He has met with success both at and before trial, having secured acquittals, dismissals and summary dispositions in a host of matters, and in persuading government authorities that no charges should be filed.
Mr. Fleisher is an experienced civil lawyer as well. He has successfully represented clients in matters brought by the SEC, FINRA and the NASD, has defended attorneys in professional liability actions, and has conducted private arbitrations and mediations in civil cases. He has also represented various financial and insurance professionals in matters before state licensing authorities.
