Professional Experience
Primary areas of practice
Family Law, Alternative Dispute Resolution/Mediation, Criminal Law and Estate Planning For 25 years, Ms. Duff has devoted her practice to assisting people in the area of family law. She has extensive experience from both sides of the bench in divorce matters, dissolutions, custody matters and other situations unique to families in crisis. Ms. Duff further is experienced in handling juvenile law matters, estate planning and criminal matters. Ms. Duff has completed all required training to allow her to act as a mediator for divorce actions, custody actions and other family law matters. This allows parties to save both time and money and avoid the uncertain outcome of submitting their cases to a Court, thus giving parties more control over their futures.
